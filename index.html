About Giselle

Tate Homes
With Giselle

Giselle Tate is a distinguished REALTOR® and Luxury Real Estate Specialist with Key Realty, serving the greater Las Vegas valley with passion, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to every client's success.

Whether you're buying your first home, upgrading to a luxury estate, or positioning a property for maximum return, Giselle brings a personalized, white-glove approach to every transaction — from initial consultation through closing day and beyond.

Her deep knowledge spans Las Vegas's most sought-after communities — from Boca Park to Silverado Ranch — giving clients an unmatched advantage in both buying and selling. The majority of her business comes exclusively through referrals, a testament to the lasting trust she builds with every client.
